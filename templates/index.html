User Guidelines

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about using ResQ during emergencies.

How do I report a disaster?

You can report a disaster by clicking the 'Submit Report' button in the navigation bar, logging in (if not already), and filling out the disaster report form with details about the incident.

What is the Send SOS feature?

The Send SOS feature allows you to send an emergency alert with your location to authorities if you're in immediate danger. Ensure you're logged in and have location services enabled.

How can I stay updated on danger zones?

Use the 'Danger Zones' link in the navigation to access real-time crowd-sourced heat maps and critical alerts about hazardous areas in your region.
